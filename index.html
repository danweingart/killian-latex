What We Do

Three Pillars of Excellence

01

Latex Compounding

Custom natural and synthetic latex compounds engineered for dipping, casting, molding, coating, and binder applications across medical, industrial, and consumer markets.

02

Dipped Goods Manufacturing

FDA-compliant manufacturing of dipped medical devices, including 510(k) ultrasound probe covers, along with custom coagulant-dipped and thin-walled products.

03

Research & Development

Comprehensive latex formulation and application development with in-house testing for viscosity, tensile strength, pH, particle size, and total solids content.

Our Heritage

45 Years of Precision Latex Manufacturing

Since 1979, Killian Latex, Inc. and Kiltex Corporation have served as trusted partners for companies requiring custom latex compounding and precision dipped goods manufacturing. As a family-owned operation based in Akron, Ohio, we bring generations of expertise and a personal commitment to every formulation we develop.

Our FDA-compliant facility and ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system ensure that every product meets the highest standards of consistency and performance.

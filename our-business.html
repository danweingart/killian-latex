Our Business

Latex compounding, dipped goods manufacturing, and research & development — backed by 45 years of expertise and ISO 9001:2015 certification.

Killian Latex, Inc.

Latex Compounding Services

For 45 years, Killian Latex has developed custom elastomeric formulations tailored to the precise requirements of our customers. We compound both natural and synthetic latex for a wide range of applications.

Natural Latex Products

Our natural latex compounds are formulated for superior performance across four primary application categories.

Dipping Compounds

High-performance natural latex dipping compounds engineered for medical devices, examination and surgical gloves, balloons, and other thin-film applications requiring exceptional elasticity and barrier properties.

Casting Compounds

Precision-formulated casting compounds for dolls, masks, toys, and specialty products. Our casting formulations deliver excellent detail reproduction, consistent cure characteristics, and reliable mechanical properties.

Molding Compounds

Natural latex molding compounds designed for architectural elements, concrete form liners, and decorative applications. Engineered for dimensional stability, tear resistance, and extended mold life.

Coating & Binder Applications

Versatile natural latex formulations for coating and binder applications across industrial and consumer markets. Our compounds provide excellent adhesion, flexibility, and environmental resistance.

Synthetic Latex Products

Our synthetic latex compounds extend the performance envelope beyond natural rubber, offering enhanced chemical resistance and specialized properties.

Dipping & Coating Compounds

Synthetic latex compounds formulated for dipping and coating applications where natural rubber sensitivity or specific chemical resistance requirements demand an alternative elastomer system.

Medical & Automotive Applications

Specialized synthetic formulations engineered for the demanding requirements of medical device manufacturing and automotive component production, including enhanced aging characteristics and controlled physical properties.

Toll Compounding Services

Killian Latex offers comprehensive toll compounding services as a contingency supplier for companies seeking cost-effective, reliable manufacturing alternatives. Whether you need backup production capacity, overflow support, or a cost-competitive alternative to in-house compounding, our facility and expertise are available to meet your requirements with consistent quality and on-time delivery.

Kiltex Corporation

Dipped Goods Manufacturing

For 40 years, Kiltex Corporation has manufactured precision dipped products in our FDA-compliant facility, serving the medical device and specialty manufacturing industries.

Medical Devices — FDA 510(k) Compliant

Our medical devices are manufactured under strict FDA 510(k) compliance, ensuring the highest standards of safety and quality for clinical applications.

Rectal Ultrasound Probe Covers

Available in 2.0 cm and 2.6 cm diameters × 20 cm length. Manufactured to meet exacting tolerances for consistent fit and reliable barrier protection during diagnostic ultrasound procedures.

Vaginal Ultrasound Probe Covers

3.5 cm diameter × 20 cm length. Precision-dipped for uniform wall thickness, providing reliable barrier protection and comfortable fit for transvaginal ultrasound examinations.

Custom Dipped Products

Beyond our standard medical device line, Kiltex manufactures custom dipped products using three primary dipping processes to meet your specific application requirements:

Coagulant Dipping

Controlled-thickness dipping process using coagulant-treated formers for products requiring uniform wall thickness and precise dimensional control.

Straight Dipping

Direct immersion dipping for applications where graduated wall thickness and simplified processing deliver optimal performance and cost efficiency.

Thin-Walled Products

Specialized thin-wall dipping techniques for applications demanding maximum sensitivity and flexibility while maintaining reliable barrier integrity.

Research & Development
